About WorldSurf

The WorldSurf surfing competition is the best one you will see in Hawaii in terms of talent, entertainment, and overall experience. The competition features 32 surfers who have qualified for the competition. There is a bracket that will have the contestants in order by a number of factors. The WorldSurf competition is 3 days long and has live entertainment every night with music guests such as Jack U, Kanye West, and The Black Keys. We have prize giveaways throughout the duration of the competition and will have a raffle draw at the end of the competition.
